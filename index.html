About Me

Hello! My name is Ryan Anders Leech, and I'm a recent graduate from MTU in Cork where I specialized in Business Information Systems (BIS), graduating with a 2.1 degree.

I am currently employed as a Software Engineer at ClickDimensions, where I work on complex marketing automation solutions. My work primarily revolves around utilizing Azure Cloud Services, Angular, TypeScript, .NET Core, Azure Storage, and both SQL & Mongo Databases. I've also gained experience in deploying and managing services through AWS and Azure Webjobs.

My tech stack is diverse, encompassing languages like C#, TypeScript, and technologies including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Microsoft Dynamics, and AngularJS. This wide-ranging skill set enables me to tackle challenges head-on and contribute effectively to various aspects of a project.
